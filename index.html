What is Curie?

Curie is a health assistant designed to provide personalized healthcare guidance and support to individuals. It leverages advanced artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms to offer a range of health-related services.

Health Monitoring:

Curie can track your vital signs, such as heart rate, blood pressure, and sleep patterns, by interfacing with wearable devices or sensors.

Medical Information:

Medical Information: It has access to a vast database of medical knowledge, allowing it to answer questions about symptoms, conditions, treatments, and medications.

Personalized Recommendations:

Curie uses your health data and preferences to provide tailored advice on diet, exercise, and lifestyle changes to help you achieve your health goals.

Medication Management:

Curie can send medication reminders and provide information about dosages and potential interactions between different medications.
